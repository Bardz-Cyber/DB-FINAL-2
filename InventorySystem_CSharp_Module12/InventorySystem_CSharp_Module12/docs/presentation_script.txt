Presentation Script - Inventory Management System in C#

Slide 1: Good day everyone. Our Module 12 final database project is an Inventory Management System developed using C# WinForms and SQLite.

Slide 2: The system helps a small business monitor products, suppliers, categories, stock levels, and stock movement transactions.

Slide 3: The main users are inventory staff and administrators. They can add products, update stock, check low stock items, and view reports.

Slide 4: This is the ER diagram. Categories and suppliers connect to products. Products connect to stock transactions and sales order items. Customers connect to sales orders.

Slide 5: The normalized tables are Categories, Suppliers, Products, Customers, StockTransactions, SalesOrders, and SalesOrderItems. The design follows third normal form.

Slide 6: The SQL scripts include table creation, sample data, and report queries such as low stock, inventory valuation, and transaction history.

Slide 7: The C# application uses a clean dark dashboard UI. It shows total products, low stock items, and total inventory value.

Slide 8: Product management supports adding, editing, deleting, searching, and viewing category and supplier information.

Slide 9: Stock transactions support stock-in and stock-out. The system prevents stock-out when quantity is not enough.

Slide 10: In conclusion, the system meets the requirements: ER diagram, normalized tables, SQL scripts, queries report, working C# system, and presentation. Thank you.
